  • Why Staking?

    Delegating your ADA allows you to monetize in a safe and easy way without loosing control of your funds.

    Think of staking more like a vote of trust. Your funds will never leave your wallet. You pledge a vote of trust of a certain value to a Stake Pool, meaning you trust that Stake Pool efficieancy to mint block. As a reward for your trust, that stake pool will share the income generated by it's delegators - you!

  • How to Delegate?

    Don't go far! Just a few clicks.

    If you're using Daedalus/Yoroi wallet you can delegate directly in your wallet site. With few clicks, find our Stake Pool using the ticker 'PACT' and choose how much you would like to delegate.

  • What then?

    Sit back and Profit

    At each epoch (every 5 days) you will receive the rewards generated by the stake pool. And that's it! no 'ifs' no 'buts'!

  • Do some good!

    Have a + Impact

    The rewards generated by the stake pool will be distributed to our delegators and the Stake Pool fees will be donated to out selected charities. To follow our performance and progress just follow us on social media.
